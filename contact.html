Frequently Asked Questions

How do I enroll in a course?

You can enroll in any course by clicking the "Add to Cart" button on the course page, then proceeding to checkout. Create an account to track your progress and access course materials.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept PayPal, bank transfers, Vodafone Cash, and cash payments at our authorized centers. All payments are secure and processed safely.

Do you offer certificates upon completion?

Yes! All our courses come with certificates of completion. Our AI Professional Certification is industry-recognized and can boost your career prospects.

Can I get a refund if I'm not satisfied?

We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee for all our courses. If you're not satisfied with the content, contact us within 30 days for a full refund.

Do you provide job placement assistance?

Yes! Our certification program includes career support, resume review, interview preparation, and connections to our partner companies actively hiring AI professionals.

Are the courses suitable for beginners?

Absolutely! We have courses for all skill levels. Our beginner-friendly courses start with the basics and gradually build up to advanced concepts. No prior AI experience required.
